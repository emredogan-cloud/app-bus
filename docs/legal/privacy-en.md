# Privacy Policy

App-Bus ("the app") respects your privacy. This policy describes what personal
data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it under KVKK (Turkey's
data-protection law) and GDPR.

## 1. Controller

App-Bus Yazılım — emre30283@gmail.com

## 2. Data we process

| Data                                                      | Purpose                                | Legal basis                            |
| --------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Email, name, password hash                                | Account creation + session management  | Performance of contract (KVKK m.5/2-c) |
| KVKK consent records (version, IP, user-agent, timestamp) | Legal obligation                       | KVKK m.5/2-a                           |
| Precise device location (only while the app is open)      | Showing nearby stops                   | Explicit consent                       |
| Push notification token                                   | Sending notifications you opted in for | Explicit consent                       |
| Crash reports (anonymized, source-mapped)                 | Stability                              | Legitimate interest                    |

## 3. Retention

- Active accounts: retained for the lifetime of the account.
- Deletion requests: 90-day hold, then PII is anonymized.
- Location queries: anonymized after 30 days.
- Crash reports never contain PII.

## 4. Where we host

Your data is processed in **AWS Frankfurt (eu-central-1)**. Any transfer outside
Turkey honors KVKK Council adequacy decisions or your explicit consent.

## 5. Your rights

Under KVKK Art. 11 / GDPR Art. 15-22 you may request:

- access, rectification, deletion, portability, restriction, or to object;
- by writing to support@app-bus.tr.

## 6. Cookies

The mobile app does not use cookies. The web version uses one strictly-necessary
session cookie; marketing cookies are loaded only with explicit consent.
